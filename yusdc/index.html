What We Do

Mirror real world assets for use within Stellar
Stellar Anchor UK mirrors real world assets on the Stellar network. We currently provide the pegged stablecoin GBPC, allowing users of Stellar to hold a representation on the Great British Pound within the ecosystem.
Each token is backed by a USD reserve, which is verifiable by viewing our distribution wallet on the network. We ensure to always have enough USD in reserve to buy back all GBPC that are issued.
