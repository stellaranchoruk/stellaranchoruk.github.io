AQUAm25 Locker (1-Year Lock)

Connect your wallet, view your AQUAm25 balance, choose a percentage or an exact amount, and sign the lock transaction. Lock start and end times are shown before signing.

Freighter browser extension supported. You can also copy the XDR or use Stellar Lab.
