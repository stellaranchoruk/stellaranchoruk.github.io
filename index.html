What We Do

Mirrasets mirrors real world assets on the Stellar network. We currently provide the pegged stablecoins GBPC, KRWC & EURC, allowing users of Stellar to hold representations of the Great British Pound, South Korean Won, and Euro within the ecosystem.


Each token is backed by a USDC reserve, which is verifiable by viewing our issuing wallets on the network. We ensure to always have enough USDC in reserve to buy back all tokens that we issue.
